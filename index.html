This is the Ping Pong game! Enter a number into the form and see all of the numbers on the screen. Any number that is divisible by 3 will be changed into 'Ping'. Numbers that are divisible by 5 will be a 'pong', and numbers that are divisible by both 3 and 5 will display PingPong.
